About the Author

The goal of "Balanced Edge" is to empower professionals to master their wellbeing in the demanding, fast-paced world they navigate daily. It seeks to inspire a transformative shift in how readers perceive and engage with their health, stress, relationships, and overall life balance. By offering compassionate guidance and actionable strategies, the book aims to help professionals reclaim vitality and harmony, reduce burnout, and sustainably enhance both personal and professional fulfilment. It aspires to be a lasting companion that fosters resilience, mindfulness, and purposeful living, enabling readers to thrive with energy, joy, and presence despite external pressures.

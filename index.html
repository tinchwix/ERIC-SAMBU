The Journey

Governance, leadership & excellence. Read More arrow_forward

Eric Sambu is a distinguished leader with over 24 years of experience, currently serving as the Managing Director of SGA Security Tanzania. His academic journey is marked by a PhD in Management (specializing in governance) and a Doctor of Business Administration (specializing in AI) from the University of Dar es Salaam, alongside multiple Master's degrees in Business Administration, Revenue Law, and Governance.

Recognized for his exceptional leadership, Eric has been named CEO of the Year for five consecutive years (2021-2025) and received the Outstanding CEO Award from the CEO Round Table of Tanzania in 2024. He is a Fellow of ICPAK and a prominent facilitator in international workshops specializing in corporate governance, risk management, and cybersecurity.

Industry Impact & SGA Security

Under Eric's stewardship, SGA Security has become East Africa's most trusted security provider, employing over 5,000 people and earning multiple ISO certifications (9001, 14001, 45001, 18788). The company has been recognized as the Best Employer and the Most Compliant Large Taxpayer by TRA.
